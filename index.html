Transparent Data Usage

We believe in complete transparency about how we access and use your data. Here's exactly what we request and why:

  • What we request: Read-only access to your Google Drive files
  • Why we need it: To scan for music files (MP3, FLAC, AAC, OGG, WAV, M4A) and stream them directly to your device
  • What we access: Only music files in your Google Drive. We scan file metadata (title, artist, album) to organize your library
  • What we DON'T access: Your documents, photos, spreadsheets, or any other non-music files. We cannot see, modify, delete, or share any of your files
  • How we use it: Music files are streamed directly from Google Drive to your device. We do not download, store, or cache your music files on our servers
  • Data storage: Any cached metadata (like playlists) is stored locally on your device only and can be cleared anytime
  • Your control: You can revoke Google Drive access at any time through your Google Account settings â†’ Security â†’ Third-party apps with account access
  • Security: All connections are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols. We use Google's secure OAuth 2.0 authentication system
