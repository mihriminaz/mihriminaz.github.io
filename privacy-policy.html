Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to ezgy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our music player application.

2. Information We Collect

ezgy is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Google Drive Access: We request access to your Google Drive to scan for and play music files. We only access files you explicitly grant permission for.
  • Music Files: We read metadata from your music files (title, artist, album) to organize your library.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Providing music playback functionality
  • Organizing and displaying your music library
  • Improving app performance and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Google Drive Integration

When you connect your Google Drive account, we use Google's OAuth 2.0 authentication system. We only request read-only access to your music files. We cannot:

  • Modify or delete your files
  • Access files outside of your music folders
  • View your personal documents or other Drive contents
  • Share your files with others

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

ezgy's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, ezgy:

  • Only uses Google user data to provide and improve the music playback functionality of the app
  • Does not use Google user data for serving advertisements, including retargeting, personalized, or interest-based advertising
  • Does not sell Google user data to third parties
  • Does not use Google user data for credit, lending, or insurance eligibility determinations
  • Does not transfer Google user data to data brokers or information resellers
  • Does not use Google user data to train artificial intelligence or machine learning models
  • Does not use Google user data to develop, improve, or train AI/ML models unrelated to the app's functionality

6. Data Storage

ezgy does not operate any servers that store your data. All music is either:

  • Streamed directly: Music plays directly from your Google Drive to your device in real-time.
  • Cached locally: Files may be temporarily cached on your device for offline playback or improved performance.

Important: We do not copy, store, or transfer any of your Google Drive files to our servers. All data remains on Google's servers or on your personal device under your control.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since ezgy does not store your data on our servers, data retention is entirely under your control:

  • Locally cached data: Any music files cached on your device can be deleted by clearing the app's cache or data through your device settings.
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • Google Drive access: You can revoke ezgy's access to your Google Drive at any time by visiting your Google Account permissions page.

We do not retain any Google user data on our systems. Once you revoke access or uninstall the app, there is no data for us to delete because we never stored it in the first place.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke Google Drive access at any time through your Google account settings
  • Clear app data and cache from your device
  • Uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All connections to Google Drive are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mihriminaz@gmail.com or visit our Support page.
